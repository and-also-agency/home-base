A Stroke of Brush Studios

  • Design
  • Development
  • WordPress
The Problem(s)
A Stroke of Brush Studios hadn't updated their website since DaVinci painted the Sistine Chapel, and wanted to modernize. The studio wanted an up-to-date, mobile-friendly site with a blog, and the ability to make ongoing contributions to the site themselves.
The Solution(s)
And Also Agency evaluated the site structure and found ways to improve navigation and user experience. We developed the site using WordPress as a content management solution, allowing the studio to post new content and articles without the assistance of a developer.
