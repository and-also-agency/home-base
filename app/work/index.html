The Challenge(s)
EdgeCore Internet Real Estate engaged with And Also Agency to execute the organization’s international brand launch as well as manage ongoing marketing efforts. The company needed a visual brand, website, social presence, media communications, print materials and advertising.
The Solution(s)

And Also Agency worked closely with EdgeCore’s leadership team to develop the company’s visual brand and overall marketing strategy. From there, &AA designed and developed edgecore.com, company brochures, and social media profiles along with geo-specific and industry advertising partnerships.
