Our Story

We've Been In Your Shoes

With 15+ years of experience working in corporate marketing, we’ve been responsible for hiring and managing multiple agency relationships. We’ve also worked with a variety of freelancers.

It Wasn't Always Great

In many instances, hiring and working with agencies and freelancers was positive. But, a good number of times we found agency costs to be exorbitant for the quality of the product as well as freelancer timelines and project management lacking.

So We Decided To Make Something Better

What if there was a company that combined the best parts of working with agencies (project management, clear timelines, responsiveness) with the best parts of working with freelancers (cost effectiveness, flexibility, and direct communication)? Well, we thought, that would be great. So, we made And Also Agency.

Expertise

We are web designers/developers/advertisers/strategists who love creating high-quality work, and seeing results. With well over 15 years of experience working on and leading corporate marketing teams on our side, we understand the criticality of choosing a strategy that not only builds impactful brand awareness, but fuels the bottom line. Give us an opportunity to make a difference for your business, and you'll see how passionate we are about doing things right.

As for the personal stuff? We have some serious wanderlust, love cats, and one of us moonlights as a tap dancing teacher.
