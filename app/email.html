FIRST HALF 2017 NEWSLETTER

It is my pleasure to introduce the Children of Rwanda newsletter! We would like to use this first issue as a way of thanking our supporters, and showing you the impact of your contributions.

It is hard to believe that it has already been three years since Children of Rwanda was registered as a charity. From humble beginnings, it has grown into a successful charity that helps educate hundreds of children and provides them and their families with vital health insurance every year. I have seen first hand the profound impact that this has on the children and families we are involved with, so I would like to thank you for making it possible.

– Robbie, CEO

Our New Board of Trustees!

Great news from the end of 2016, Children of Rwanda confirmed an impressive Board of Trustees. These six individuals bring a world of experience to, guiding toward a future where we reach more children through our current programmes and implement new programmes. Keep an eye on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to find out more!

This Year's Impact

As the organization continues to grow, we are able to take on more and more children every year. This year, with only £12,500, Children of Rwanda enabled 190 children to return or remain in their local schools and will provide health insurance to 845 individuals.

Why It Matters

Children who attend school are prone to:

• Live healthier lives
• Understand nutrition
• Improve farming
• Have job opportunities
• Have healthier children

Girls who attend school are more likely to:

• Marry later in life
• Have fewer children
• Earn 15-20% more in life for every additional year of secondary schooling
